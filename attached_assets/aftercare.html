Tattoo Aftercare

Proper aftercare ensures optimal healing and long‑term appearance of your tattoo.

First 24 Hours

  • Leave the bandage on for 2–4 hours (or as advised).
  • Wash hands, then gently clean with lukewarm water and mild, fragrance‑free soap. Pat dry with a clean paper towel.
  • Apply a thin layer of aftercare ointment recommended by your artist.

Days 2–14

  • Wash gently twice daily; apply a thin moisturizer as needed.
  • Do not pick or scratch; light flaking/itching is normal.
  • Avoid submerging (baths, pools, ocean) and avoid direct sun.
  • Wear clean, loose clothing over the tattooed area.

Long‑Term Care

  • Use sunscreen (SPF 30+) after healed to prevent fading.
  • Keep the skin moisturized to maintain vibrancy.
  • Contact the studio if you notice unusual redness, swelling, or signs of infection.
