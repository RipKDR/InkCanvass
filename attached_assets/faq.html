Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept walk-ins?

We primarily work by appointment. Same-day slots may be available — please call ahead to check availability.

What is the minimum age?

18+ with valid ID. We do not tattoo minors.

How much is the deposit?

A 30% non‑refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. This is credited to your final price.

Can I bring reference images?

Yes. Upload links or files during booking; bring additional references to your consultation for discussion.
